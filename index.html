What is Masrofy?
Masrofy (مصروفي) means "my expenses" in Arabic. It's a simple, offline-first expense tracker designed to help you understand where your money actually goes — and spot the leaks before they drain you.
Features
  • Track daily expenses by category
  • Visual spending breakdown & insights
  • Export & import data as CSV
  • Works offline — your data stays on your device
  • Arabic & English language support
  • Installable as a mobile app (PWA)
